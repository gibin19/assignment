LOLIPOP

Lollipop is a popular candy treat characterized by its iconic round shape mounted on a stick.

HOW IT MAKES

Typically made from sugar, flavoring agents, and food coloring, lollipops come in a variety of flavors such as fruit, sour, and even novelty flavors like cotton candy or bubblegum.

special

The sweet confection is enjoyed by people of all ages and is often associated with childhood nostalgia and fun. Lollipops are commonly found at candy stores, supermarkets, and as party favors, making them a delightful treat for special occasions or just a simple indulgence for a sweet tooth.
